About Us

Welcome to the official site of the unknown but totally worthy band A Chemical of Romance. We are an up and coming Rock band based out of Sheridan, Wyoming. We make songs for those who cant take no for an answer, we make rebel music for those who won't stand by and let the government brainwash us, we make music for those uptight parents who don't let their kids express themselves, but most importantly we make music for you!
